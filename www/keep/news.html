News

May 20, 2008

  1. New siRNA search pattern has been added to website.
  2. Up-to-date SNPs for the siRNAs in human and rat have been added.
  3. For each siRNA candidate, a list of potential miRNA targets is shown.

August 6 2004

  1. More sensitive WU BLAST has been added.
  2. Users can BLAST NCBI RefSeq and Ensembl Transcript databases in additional to UniGene database.
  3. Rat in addition to human and mouse database are added.
  4. "Best hit" has been removed. Instead the program will search our local databases to find corresponding Gene ID for your target sequence's accession/gi number.
  5. In the BLAST result page, you can further filter out non-specific siRNAs to reduce off-target effects.

May 21 2004

  1. We support Safari browser in addition to Internet Explorer and Netscape
  2. The positions 1-20 of the antisense siRNA are used for BLASTing. Alignment of BLAST results is shown at 5'-> 3' direction.

Jan.30 2004

  1. Thermodynamic values for each siRNA are calculated based on the papers published at Zamore's lab and by Khvorova et.al.
  2. Filters: siRNAs can be filtered with any number of consecutive G/C.
  3. Sort function have been added. Users can sort their siRNAs by many criterias: such as thermodynamic values, BLAST results, GC% etc.
  4. Better format in the result page. For each siRNA, all its BLAST results are condensed into one file and linked to it.

    5. Click on FAQ to see more information.

Sep. 15 2003

  1. siRNA patterns: NARN17YNN and NANN17YNN have been removed.
  2. Filters: 'no more than 3 of the same base in a row' has been removed. 'exclude a run of 4 or more T or A in a row' has been added to the website.
  3. Results: more genes with similar alignments with siRNA are included.

    4. For more information, please refer to the links in the user input page.

Feb. 26 2003

  1. You can input either a raw sequence or a fasta format sequence in the sequence field.
  2. You can now receive your results from the web directly and/or from email. Email is no longer the only option to receive results.
  3. Bugs in delivering some email results have been corrected.
