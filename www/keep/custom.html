How to describe your oligo pattern?

  • NC-IUB nucleic acid codes are accepted. 
    • A --> adenosine      K --> G T (keto)          B --> C G T        N --> A G C T (any)
C --> cytidine       M --> A C (amino)         D --> A G T
G --> guanine        R --> A G (purine)        H --> A C T
T --> thymidine      S --> C G (strong)        V --> A C G
                     W --> A T (weak)
                     Y --> C T (pyrimidine)
  • Ambiguities can also be indicated by listing the acceptable nucleotides for a given position, between square parentheses `[ ]'. For example: [TC] stands for T or C.
  • Repetition of an element of the pattern can be indicated by following that element with a numerical value.
Examples:

GCN19[CT]2

This searches for GC,


followed by any 19 bases,
 

then by "C" or "T" 2 times.


N4AN6TN2HN5WN2

This searches for A at position 5,


and T or U at position 12,


and a base other than G at position 15,


and A or T or U at position 21.
